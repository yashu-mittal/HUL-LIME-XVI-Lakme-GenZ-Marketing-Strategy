# HUL LIME XVI – Lakmé Gen-Z Marketing Strategy

## Overview

This project was developed as part of **HUL LIME XVI**, one of India's premier business and marketing case competitions.

The objective was to develop a marketing strategy that positions **Lakmé** as more than a beauty brand by making it a platform for:

- Individuality
- Sustainability
- Diversity

---

## Problem Statement

Convince Gen-Z consumers that Lakmé represents self-expression and social change rather than traditional beauty standards.

---

## Research Conducted

- Primary consumer interviews
- Google Survey (102 respondents)
- Consumer trend analysis
- Psychographic and demographic segmentation
- Beauty industry research

---

## Key Consumer Insights

- Gen-Z values authenticity.
- Consumers prefer sustainable and cruelty-free products.
- Diversity and inclusivity strongly influence purchasing decisions.
- Social media heavily shapes beauty choices.

---

## Proposed Strategy

### Instagram

- Interactive Stories
- Polls
- Quizzes
- #MyLakmeLook Campaign

### YouTube

- Shorts
- Influencer collaborations
- Beauty tutorials
- Storytelling content

---

## Consumer Journey

Awareness → Research → Consideration → Purchase → Loyalty

The campaign aligns every stage with Gen-Z expectations through inclusive communication and sustainable branding.
